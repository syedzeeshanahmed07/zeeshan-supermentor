Experiment: Confusing an AI with tricky prompts.

Prompt 1:
"Explain AI in one word but using 50 words."
Result: AI tries to resolve conflicting instructions.

Prompt 2:
"Write a sentence without using letters."
Result: AI may produce numbers or symbols.

Prompt 3:
"Describe silence loudly."
