Weak vs Strong Prompts

1. Resume
Weak: Write a resume.
Strong: Create a professional resume for a computer science student with Python and ML skills.

2. Business Idea
Weak: Give business idea.
Strong: Suggest a profitable AI-based startup idea for students.

3. Study Plan
Weak: Study plan for exams.
Strong: Create a 7-day study plan for machine learning basics.

Conclusion:
Strong prompts give clearer instructions and better AI responses.
